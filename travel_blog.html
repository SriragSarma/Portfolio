My Travel Blog

Experiences from places I have visited over time.

Hyderabad Trip

Hyderabad is a vibrant city that blends tradition and modern development. Visiting Charminar and exploring local food was a memorable experience.

Amritsar Trip

Amritsar is a city rich in history, culture, and spirituality. Visiting the Golden Temple was a peaceful and unforgettable experience, and the city’s heritage left a deep impression on me.

Guwahati (GAU) Trip

Guwahati is a beautiful city surrounded by nature and the Brahmaputra River. The calm environment, scenic views, and cultural diversity made the trip refreshing and memorable.
